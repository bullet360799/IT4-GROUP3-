MYSELF

INTRODUCTION,

My name is Kelvin Kusime, and I am 18 years old. I was born on 8th May 2007 in Akwatia, a beautiful town known for its peaceful environment and friendly people. I come from a loving family. my parents, Mr. and Mrs. Kusime, have always been my greatest source of motivation and support. I have three wonderful siblings: Tracy, Faddey, and Helena, who make our home lively and full of laughter. I completed my secondary education at St. Martins Secondary School, where I developed not only my academic abilities but also my leadership and social skills. My time there taught me the value of hard work, discipline, and teamwork.

Currently, I am a student at the University of Professional Studies, Accra (UPSA), where I am pursuing higher education to build a successful career. I am passionate about learning and eager to explore opportunities that will help me grow both academically and personally. Outside of academics, I enjoy listening to music, reading motivational books, playing football, and spending time with friends. I also love exploring new ideas and keeping up with technology and current trends. These hobbies help me stay creative, active, and balanced. In the future, I aspire to make a positive impact in society by using my knowledge and skills to solve real-world problems. I believe that with determination, humility, and faith, I can achieve anything I set my mind to.

My Hobbies

Everyone has hobbies that make their lives more interesting and enjoyable. Hobbies are activities we love to do during our free time, and they help us relax, learn new things, and develop our talents. I also have several hobbies that make my life fun and meaningful. One of my favorite hobbies is listening to music. I enjoy different types of music, especially inspirational and calm songs. Music helps me relax when I feel tired or stressed, and it keeps me motivated when studying. Another hobby I enjoy is playing football. Football keeps me active and healthy, and it also teaches me teamwork and discipline. I often play with my friends after school or during weekends, and it is always fun. I also love reading books, especially storybooks and motivational books. Reading helps me improve my vocabulary, gain new knowledge, and understand life better. Sometimes, I spend my quiet evenings reading inspiring stories that encourage me to work hard and chase my dreams. In addition, I like drawing and watching educational videos. Drawing allows me to express my creativity, while educational videos help me learn about new ideas, science, and technology. All these hobbies make me a more balanced and happy person. They help me relax after studying and give me the strength to focus on my goals. I believe hobbies are an important part of life because they help us discover our interests and develop our hidden talents.
